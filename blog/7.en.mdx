I have to admit, I can no longer live without an AI writing companion. For me, the two most important features are: first, catching typos—a task that is both labor-intensive and nearly impossible to perfect, making it ideal for AI to handle.

The other is translation, since I've added bilingual content to my blog. I usually express my thoughts in my native language, then have AI translate them into English. After that, I read through the translation to see if anything doesn't quite match my intended meaning. Models these days do an excellent job at this—far better than I could as a non-native writer.

So, I added this AI writing companion feature to [Input Pub](https://inputpub.com). You can select a passage (or select all), trigger the AI companion, and tell it how you'd like it revised. Check out the demo:

![1.00](https://my-imgs.inputpub.com/1782894549062-zt5qwf-inputpub-ai-en.gif)

Currently, we support OpenAI-compatible APIs. Your API key is stored entirely in your browser's local storage—feel free to give it a try.

### Other Use Cases

* Expand: Turn a 20-character self-criticism into 1,000 words 🐶

* Add links to less common concepts or tools mentioned in the article

### Another AI-Related Feature in Input Pub

When you click "Publish," you'll see a ChatGPT option (you can also configure other AI models). Clicking it will bring your entire article into ChatGPT's input box, making it easy to ask for revisions or do anything else you'd like with the content.
